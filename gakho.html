Gá Kho (Vietnamese Caramelized Chicken)

Gá kho (Vietnamese caramelized chicken) is a delicious, bite sized meal that brings together sweet and savory flavors. It is best served with jasmine rice and veggies such as yu choy on the side.

Best of all, it doesn't take long to cook and is great for beginners in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs (chicken breast can also work)
  • Fish sauce (can substitute with soy sauce)
  • Palm sugar (substitute with brown sugar)
  • Rice vinegar
  • Coconut soda
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Ginger
  • Black pepper
  • Thai chillies
  • Scallion or cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  • In a saucepan, add chopped palm sugar and let it melt over medium heat until caramelized.
  • Stir to even out color, be careful not to let burn. Let the sugar caramelize until dark brown.
  • Turn off the heat and add the coconut soda.
  • Turn the heat back on to warm the sugar. Then add fish sauce and rice vinegar.
  • Heat a wok over medium-high heat. Add oil, and cook the garlic, shallots, ginger and black pepper until fragrant.
  • Push everything to the side and add the chicken in an even layer. Cover it with the cooked vegetables and let cook for about two minutes.
  • Once the bottom is golden brown, the dish is complete.
