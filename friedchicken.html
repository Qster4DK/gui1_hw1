Fried Chicken Tenders

American fried chicken tenders are a delicious and easy meal to serve for lunch and dinner for any occasion. They are great to serve to guests as almost everyone likes their simple flavor.

The seasonings are very customizable and this dish can even be made spicy. It goes great with many sides such as mac and cheese or corn on the cob.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup pancake mix
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cajun seasoning
  • 2 pounds chicken breasts
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  • Cut chicken into strips.
  • In a bowl, whisk the egg and buttermilk.
  • Marinate chicken in this mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  • In a separate bowl, combine the pancake mix and seasonings.
  • Dip the chicken in the dry mixture.
  • In a Dutch oven or frying pan, heat frying oil to about 375Â°.
  • Fry chicken, a few pieces at a time, for 2-3 minutes on each side or until no longer pink.
  • Drain on paper towels.
